Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA)

This Non-Disclosure Agreement (the "Agreement") is entered into as of [Date], by and between: Disclosing Party: [Your Full Legal Name or Company Name] [Your Address] [City, State, ZIP Code] [Email Address] [Phone Number] Receiving Party: [Recipient's Full Legal Name or Company Name] [Recipient's Address] [City, State, ZIP Code] [Recipient's Email Address] [Recipient's Phone Number]

1. Definition of Confidential Information

For the purposes of this Agreement, "Confidential Information" shall include any non-public information, including but not limited to trade secrets, business plans, financial data, customer lists, and any proprietary information disclosed by the Disclosing Party to the Receiving Party.

2. Obligations of Receiving Party

The Receiving Party agrees to:

  • Keep the Confidential Information confidential and not disclose it to any third party without the prior written consent of the Disclosing Party.
  • Use the Confidential Information solely for the purpose of [Specify Purpose, e.g., discussing potential business collaboration] and not for any other purpose.
  • Take all reasonable measures to protect the confidentiality of the Confidential Information and prevent unauthorized disclosure.

3. Exclusions from Confidential Information

The obligations of confidentiality do not apply to information that:

  • Is or becomes publicly available through no fault of the Receiving Party.
  • Is independently developed by the Receiving Party without reference to the Confidential Information.
  • Is rightfully obtained by the Receiving Party from a third party without any obligation of confidentiality.

4. Duration of Confidentiality Obligations

The Receiving Party's obligations of confidentiality shall continue for a period of [Specify Duration, e.g., two (2) years] from the date of disclosure of the Confidential Information.

5. Return or Destruction of Confidential Information

Upon the Disclosing Party's written request, or the termination of discussions between the parties, the Receiving Party shall promptly return or, at the Disclosing Party's option, destroy all copies of the Confidential Information.

6. Governing Law and Jurisdiction

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of [Your Jurisdiction]. Any disputes arising under or in connection with this Agreement shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts in [Your Jurisdiction].
