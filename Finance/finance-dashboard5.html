Section 3 - Terms and Conditions for 80G Receipt

1. Eligibility

This receipt is issued only to individuals and entities eligible for tax deduction under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act, 1961.

2. Donation Details:

The receipt acknowledges a voluntary donation made to the Halal India Research Council, a recognized charitable organization.

3. Receipt Validity

This receipt is valid only if it contains the following details:

Name and address of the donor

o PAN of the donor

o Amount donated (in figures and words)

o Date of donation

o Receipt number

o Signature and seal of the authorized signatory of the Halal India Research council

4. Non Refundable

Donations once made are non-refundable under any circumstances.

5. Tax Deduction

The donation is eligible for tax deduction under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act, 1961. The extent of deduction available may be subject to changes in tax laws. Donors are advised to consult their tax advisors for specific details.

6. Purpose of Donation

The donated amount will be used for the charitable activities and projects undertaken by the Halal India Research Council as per its mandate.

7. Duplicate Receipts

Duplicate receipts can be issued upon request, provided sufficient proof of the original donation is submitted.

8. Changes in Information

Donors must inform the Halal India Research Council promptly of any changes in their contact information or PAN.

9. Usage of Donor Information

The Halal India Research Council respects donor privacy and will not disclose any personal information to third parties without prior consent, except as required by law.

10. Governing Law

 This receipt and the terms and conditions herein shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of India. Any disputes arising from this receipt shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts in India.
